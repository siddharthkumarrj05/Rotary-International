    Welcome to the London Community Awards!

    Celebrating the Heart and Soul of Our Community

    In the vibrant, ever-changing landscape of Greater London, countless individuals, charities, and community groups weave rich threads of compassion, innovation, and resilience every day. The London Community Awards, proudly orchestrated by Rotary in London, seek to honour these unsung heroes. These awards shine a spotlight on those tirelessly working towards enriching our communities, making a difference in lives, and fostering an environment of support and growth.

    Nominate and Apply Now

    We believe that in every corner of Greater London, there are stories of extraordinary dedication and service waiting to be told. If you know someone who embodies the spirit of community service, or if you are part of an organisation making a significant impact, we invite you to nominate them for the London Community Awards. Alternatively, you can apply directly to showcase your contribution to the community.

    Join us in this extraordinary event that not only acknowledges remarkable contributions within our community but also inspires us all to play our part in making Greater London a place of compassion, innovation, and unity. Let's come together to applaud the dedication and achievements of those who make our community a better place for everyone.

    Why a Voluntary Group's Touch Is Special?

    These awards, organised by a voluntary group, resonate deeply with a spirit of altruism and community solidarity. Free from commercial influences, our recognition stems purely from a desire to celebrate and uplift those who selflessly contribute to the welfare of others. This voluntary basis ensures that our awards maintain heartfelt authenticity, highlighting true community service and the profound impact it has on our society.

    The 2024 Rotary International London Community Awards - 2023 during the tenure of RI President Gordon McInally is scheduled for 9th & 10th February 2024 in London, England. February is also Rotary’s Peace and Conflict Resolution month.

This Conference will showcase how “Rotary brings lasting change to the world, one restored or newly created hope at a time."

    Rotary in London: A Pillar of Community Service

    Rotary in London, a vital part of the global Rotary network, is ideally positioned to host these awards. With over a century of unwavering commitment to community service, Rotary members are neighbours, friends, and leaders who envision a world where people unite for collective action and positive change. Our extensive experience in promoting peace, fighting disease, supporting education, and protecting the environment aligns perfectly with the ethos of these awards, making us a fitting organiser of this significant event.

    The London Community Awards are designed to:

    1. Recognise and celebrate the invaluable contributions of local heroes in Greater London.
    2. Showcase the rich diversity of our community and the inspiring work being done by individuals from all backgrounds.
    3. Honour those who go above and beyond in supporting their neighbours and positively impacting the lives of others.
    4. Promote community engagement, collaboration, and unity, highlighting the collective effort's power.
    5. Raise awareness of exceptional initiatives and projects, inspiring others to contribute to community development.
    6. Foster a sense of pride and appreciation for our local area, celebrating the positive impact within our community.
